WEM Photo Contest

The Wisconsin Engineer Magazine hosts an annual photo contest every fall! See below for submission information and past winners!

Submissions are now closed for the 16th Annual Wisconsin Engineer Magazine Photo Contest. Please check back in September 2024 for submissions to the 17th Annual Photo Contest!

Overview of contest rules (Download)

    BASIC SUBMISSIONS GUIDELINES FOR PHOTOGRAPHS

  • 1. The contests are open for online submissions only, through the WEM Google Form.
  • 2. Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses.
  • 3. Photos submitted must be at least 640 pixels on the shorter side, and no more than 2000 pixels on the longer side. Images should be no larger than 2MB. Photos must be in JPEG format.
  • 4. You may submit up to three entries.
  • 5. No one person can win more than one category (max award of 1 per person).
  • 6. You are required to provide a unique title & description for each image submitted.
  • 7. The same photo can be submitted for more than one contest. However, previous winning photos may not be submitted and will not be considered in the contest.
  • 8. All submitted photos must have no border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, identifying marks, or any other visible references and/or marks on the image.
  • 9. Basic editing, including color enhancement, the use of filters, and cropping of the photo(s) is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the photo(s).
  • 10. Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions, and/or manipulations, and the adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is prohibited.
  • 11. Every image uploaded is subject to a moderation process before it becomes visible on the contest page. WEM reserves the right to assess and disregard any submitted photo at our discretion.
  • 12. Photos that portray or otherwise include inappropriate and/or offensive content, including provocative nudity, violence, human rights and/or environmental violation, and/or any other contents deemed to be contrary to the law are strictly prohibited and will be immediately discarded.
  • 13. A participant who submits any such photos may be permanently banned from participating in any future contests.

    THE JUDGING

  • 1. WEM will appoint a judge/judges for each contest. This will comprise of members of the editorial team, with the exception of the decision of Miscellaneous winners.
  • 2. For the Reader’s Choice, the general public will select the winning photos via social media. Specifics about decisions for the category Reader’s Choice are determined by the Social Media and Public Relations department and are subject to change by the department discretion.
  • 3. The judging panel shall assess and determine the winning Photos. The results and the winners will be announced on the website and on social media.
  • 4. The decision of WEM’s judging panel will be final and binding on all Participants in respect to all matters relating to the Contest.
  • 5. WEM reserves the right to call for original JPEG or RAW files for the purpose of authentication. An image may be disregarded if this information cannot be provided.
