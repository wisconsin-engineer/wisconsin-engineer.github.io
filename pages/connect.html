The Wisconsin Engineer Magazine staff publishes print issues twice-yearly, once at the conclusion of the Spring semester and once at conclusion of the Fall semester.

Annual subscriptions for the Wisconsin Engineer are $20. Distribution lists are compiled at the time of publication, and therefore your subscription must be active at the time of publication (beginning of May, beginning of December) to receive that respective issue of the Wisconsin Engineer.

Please note that the arrival of your magazine depends on the postal office and it may take two weeks or more for the magazine to reach your doorstep. If you have any questions about your subscription, please contact us via the “Contact Us” page.
